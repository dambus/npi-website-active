# Sitemap

## V1 Public Navigation

1. `Home` `/`
   Purpose: give a fast, credible overview of NPI, its services, delivery approach, and selected proof points.
2. `About` `/about`
   Purpose: explain who NPI is, what kind of organization stands behind the work, and why clients can trust the team.
3. `Services` `/services`
   Purpose: present the main service groups in a concise, structured way without turning the site into a deep brochure.
4. `References` `/references`
   Purpose: act as the strongest proof-of-capability area through project listings, filtering, and future detail pages.
5. `Contact` `/contact`
   Purpose: give a direct path to inquiry, contact details, and location information.

## V1 Structure Notes

- Public navigation stays intentionally shallow and simple.
- `News` remains a v1 module, not a standalone main navigation page.
- Homepage may surface a small news snippet later, but the primary focus stays on services and references.
- `/admin` exists outside public navigation and remains intentionally hidden from the public site.

## Future Extensibility

The following items are expected extension points, but they are not part of v1 navigation:

- dedicated `News` listing and article detail pages
- individual reference detail pages if routing expands beyond the listing-first release
- careers, certifications, or downloadable company profile pages
- multi-language support if business need justifies it later

## IA Rationale

- `Home` exists to orient first-time visitors quickly and set a strong professional tone.
- `About` exists to add organizational credibility beyond visual branding alone.
- `Services` exists to clarify offer structure for potential clients and partners.
- `References` exists because project proof is the most persuasive content on this site.
- `Contact` exists to shorten the path from interest to inquiry.
