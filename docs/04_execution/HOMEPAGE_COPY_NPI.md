Engineering and design for oil, gas and process industry

## Text
Neopetrol Inženjering is a Serbian engineering company specialized in the design and development of industrial facilities in the oil, gas and process industries.

Founded in 2008 in Novi Sad, the company provides engineering, consulting and technical documentation services across all phases of project development — from conceptual studies to detailed design and construction support.

Our partners include national energy companies, government institutions and international engineering firms involved in complex industrial projects.

# ENGINEERING SERVICES

## Section title
Engineering services

## Text
Neopetrol Inženjering provides engineering and design services for industrial facilities and energy infrastructure.

Depending on the project phase, our work includes:

• Conceptual design and feasibility studies  
• Front End Engineering Design (FEED)  
• Basic engineering design  
• Detailed design for construction  
• Technical documentation and engineering studies  
• Engineering support during construction

Our engineers regularly cooperate with investors, EPC contractors and international engineering companies.
