# Homepage Blueprint

## Section Order

1. Hero
2. Quick facts
3. Services snapshot
4. Process / journey from concept to execution
5. Featured references
6. Quality / technology / software block
7. Contact CTA
8. Optional news snippet

## Section Definitions

| Section name | Objective | What the user should understand | Approximate content length | Suggested UI pattern | Data source |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hero | Set the tone and explain what NPI does in one glance. | NPI works on serious industrial engineering and delivery work, not generic corporate consulting. | Short headline, one supporting paragraph, two CTAs | Full-width hero with clear message and action pair | `static` |
| Quick facts | Add fast proof points without forcing the user to read paragraphs. | NPI has concrete capacity, sector relevance, and execution focus. | Three to five compact fact items | Number/fact strip or compact cards | `static` |
| Services snapshot | Summarize the offer before the user dives into the Services page. | NPI covers a clear service set across engineering and delivery support. | Three to six short service summaries | Grid of service cards | `static` |
| Process / journey | Show how NPI supports work from concept through execution. | The company can support more than one project phase and works in a structured way. | Four concise steps | Horizontal or vertical process timeline | `static` |
| Featured references | Bring proof close to the top half of the homepage. | NPI has relevant project experience and can demonstrate it with real examples. | Two to four featured items | Reference cards or compact carousel-free grid | `supabase-driven` |
| Quality / technology / software | Reinforce technical credibility and disciplined delivery. | NPI combines engineering discipline with modern tools and quality standards. | Short intro plus three supporting points | Split content block with supporting badges or cards | `static` |
| Contact CTA | Give the user a low-friction next step after trust is built. | Reaching out is straightforward and expected. | One short paragraph with primary CTA | Strong closing CTA band | `static` |
| Optional news snippet | Leave room for timely updates without distracting from the main story. | News exists as a supporting signal, not the main reason to visit. | One to three brief items | Small teaser row | `supabase-driven` |

## Pacing Notes

- The homepage should feel concise, corporate, and visually paced.
- Each section should do one clear job and then hand off to the next.
- References should appear early enough to build trust, but only after the visitor understands what NPI does.
